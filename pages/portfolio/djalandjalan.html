Overview

This is a project that I did together with 4 other colleagues, the composition is Human Resource & Finance, Marketing, Business Development, Designer and Researcher, Front-End, Back-End. And my role here is Designer and Researcher.

The most basic initial idea is that we want to create a traveling medium for Indonesia.

After looking around, we got a hypothesis that emerged from a colleague who got a complaint from a friend who likes traveling. He stated that when traveling, it is very difficult to match schedules with his friends so he needs other people to travel together. However, when searching the Internet, he did not find the specific medium he needed.

We then made the idea into a hypothesis "is a traveling medium that brings together other people needed in Indonesia?"

The Process

1. Research Hypothesis

In order to determine the feasibility of this hypothesis, we conducted quantitative data collection methods with respondents selected by purposive sampling with the criteria of individuals who travel every 3 to 6 months. The purpose of this data collection is to determine whether the hypothesis being tested is valid or rejected and at the same time find out other things considered by the market. And the result is a Valid Hypothesis.

And we got 106 respondents for hypothesis validation. Here are some of the core questions of quantitative validation that lead us into the user flow and wireframe processes

2. User Flow and Wireframe

After the hypothesis was proven valid, I proceeded to create a user flow and wireframe. And then I also formulated what features will be displayed according to input from my friends in marketing and business development.
